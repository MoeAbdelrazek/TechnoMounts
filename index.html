Measurement and account architecture

How the TechnoMounts stack was wired for clean data and optimisation

A strong performance system is only possible when the accounts behind it are wired correctly. Below is a summary of how Shopify, GA4, Google Ads, Merchant Center, Google Search Console, Amazon, email tools, Apollo, and CRM were aligned so that every campaign is measurable.

Acquisition, measurement, and retention map

This overview follows the real TechnoMounts setup: Shopify as the commerce engine, GA4 as the source of truth for events, and Google Ads, Merchant Center, email platforms, Apollo, and Amazon layered on top.

Acquisition, paid and organic
  • Google Ads: Performance Max, Shopping, and Search campaigns linked to GA4.
  • Google Merchant Center: feed built from Shopify with optimised titles and images.
  • Google Search Console: property connected to Shopify domain for query and page data.
  • LinkedIn and content channels driving additional traffic into landing pages.
Measurement, analytics and events
  • GA4 property attached to Shopify store with key events defined.
  • Events such as add to cart, begin checkout, purchase, and lead form submissions tracked.
  • GA4conversions shared with Google Ads for Smart Bidding optimisation.
  • Looker Studio dashboard built on GA4 and Google Search Console for reporting.
Retention and sales operations
  • Email automation through Shopify and connected platforms for welcome and abandon flows.
  • Apollo.io sequences for B2B outreach and enrichment of company level data.
  • HubSpot or similar CRM for longer cycle B2B accounts.
  • Amazon listing groundwork aligned with Shopify data for marketplace expansion.
