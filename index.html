Search engine optimisation and analytics foundation

From baseline visibility to strong early ranking in key niches

Month 1 delivered a complete search engine optimisation baseline: Google Search Console, Google Analytics 4, and a Looker Studio dashboard were aligned with TechnoMounts landing pages and key products.

Technical clean-up (titles, meta descriptions, alt text, internal linking, and schema) moved core pages from a score around 62 out of 100 to the 90–100 out-of-100 range in major auditing tools, which is consistent with top-performing pages in this category.
