Execution Timeline – Oct 27 to Dec 4

Work was sequenced deliberately so that measurement, content, visual assets and campaign structure were built in parallel.

Week 1
Technical audit and baseline

Audited Shopify, GA4, Merchant Center and product content. Identified tracking duplication, missing policy clarity, image issues and inconsistent category positioning.

Week 2
Measurement and feed repairs

Consolidated GA4 to a single clean implementation, removed duplicate scripts and resolved Merchant Center warnings, enabling accurate funnels and stronger Shopping eligibility.

Week 3
Landing pages, email system and assets

Built multi segment landing pages, long form descriptions, structured FAQs and Shopify Email templates. Created and organised a reusable asset library inside the Assets folder.

Week 4
Performance Max structure and readiness

Prepared a Performance Max structure mapped to product groups and landing pages, with clean conversion signals and projected ROAS based on actual engagement and intent.

SEO, UX and Content Improvements

The aim was to make TechnoMounts behave like a category leader. Content was restructured into clear sections and aligned with search intent for premium tablet mounting solutions in the UAE and GCC.

  • Product pages structured into overview, why choose it, ideal use cases, technical highlights and FAQs.
  • Image sizes, alt text and file names aligned with SEO and paid media quality guidelines.
  • Internal links refined between collections, landing pages and key SKUs.
  • Brand voice positioned around stability, security, compatibility and premium finish.

UX refinements ensured that the site looks and feels consistent with the new visual direction and can be reused across email and landing pages.

  • Equalised feature image grids, reduced white gaps and improved mobile rendering.
  • Warranty, return and refund policies written clearly and linked from key templates.
  • Reusable content blocks created for future landing pages and campaigns.

Analytics and Measurement Quality

Measurement integrity was critical before scaling paid traffic. GA4 was cleaned and aligned with Shopify native tracking.

  • Duplicate GA4 tags removed from checkout scripts and theme code.
  • Standard ecommerce events such as view item, add to cart and purchase validated for single firing.
  • Primary conversions configured to support automated bidding once campaigns scale.

A Looker Studio dashboard was built around Google Search Console and GA4 data to track SEO and engagement trends.

  • Visibility by country, query and landing page monitored for ongoing SEO work.
  • Engagement rates and event depth tracked by channel to assess future budget allocation.
  • Baseline performance snapshot captured before paid media launch.

Google Ads Performance Max and Merchant Center

A full Performance Max structure was planned to align with the improved feed and landing pages.

  • Asset groups defined around product families such as anti theft tablet floor stands, desktop mounts and hospitality solutions.
  • Sitelinks mapped to new landing pages so that queries land on the most relevant context.
  • Clean conversion actions selected from GA4 for automated bidding strategies.

Merchant Center quality was strengthened to support both organic and paid Shopping placements.

  • Resolved disapprovals by completing attributes, clarifying policies and refining imagery.
  • Product titles aligned with intent for tablet stands, kiosks and display solutions in the UAE.
  • Product types separated for clearer reporting and better future bid control.

Conclusion

In just over one month, TechnoMounts moved from a basic ecommerce presence to a brand with strong organic signals, premium positioning and a measurement layer ready for serious performance marketing.

The next steps are to scale Performance Max and search campaigns using the cleaned conversion signals, iterate on landing pages with structured A/B testing and expand email automation based on the strongest performing verticals.
